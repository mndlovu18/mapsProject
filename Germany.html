Breakfast - Farmer's Omelet

The Farmer's Omelet, or Farmer's Breakfast, is a German-styled omelet that is typically served at breakfast, but can be enjoyed at almost any meal. It is made from bacon, sausage, potatoes, cheese, and of course, eggs.

Lunch - Spaetzle

Spaetzle is a type of potato/pasta/dumpling combination, similar to the potato-based pasta Gnocchi. It is typically served with cheese or meat topping, or as a side to a larger dish.

Spaetzle Recipe

Dinner - Schnitzel

Schnitzel is the name for any type of meat that has been pounded thin, breaded, and fried. It is typically served with sides such as cabbage, and a bit of lemon juice.

Dessert - Black Forest Cake

Black Forest Cake is a decadent cake named for the Black Forest, and is known for the delicious combination of chocolate and cherry liquer.
