Breakfast: Chlebíčky

In Slovakia, breakfast is considered a savory dish. Popular choices are breakfast sausage and chlebíčky, which are small open-faced sandwiches. Click on the image for the recipe.

Lunch: Soup and a Main Course

Goulash is widely consisdered the national dish of Hungary, but many Slavic countries have their own version. This beef and pork stew is cooked in a large pot and always eaten with a side of bread. Most families have their own recipe that has been passed down for generations. Click on the image for the recipe.

Dinner: Bryndzové Halušky or Sheep Cheese Dumplings

Often called the "National Dish of Slovakia," Bryndzové Halušky are a type of potato-dumpings served with a traditional sheep cheese and bits of fried bacon. Click on the image for the recipe.

Dessert: Makový Koláč

Makový Koláč is a sweet poppy-seed roll. Poppy seeds are a popular and delicious filling in many Czech and Slovak desserts, and poppy seeds are a main export of that region. While dessert is not a popular meal in Slovakia, makový koláč is often made on holidays or served to children and guests as a sign of hospitality. Click on the image for the recipe.
