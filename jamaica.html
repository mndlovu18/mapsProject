Famous Breakfast Dish: Johnny Cakes

Johnny cakes are a popular pastry that can be eaten for breakfast in Jamaica. Some people eat them with honey and some people eat them with more savory ingredients like; onions, tomatoes, and some form of meat. Click on the picture to try the recipe!

Famous Lunch Dish: Jamaican Beef Patties

Jamaican Beef Patties are a delicious entree to have during the day in Jamaica. They can be made with beef or vegetables. The patties can be spicy depending on where you get them in Jamaica but that doesn’t make them any less satisfying. Click on the picture to try the recipe!

Famous Supper Dish: Jerk Chicken with Rice and Peas

Jerk chicken is mostly famous for the sauce and spices that are put into the chicken. To compliment the chicken, it is common for people to eat rice and peas, and maybe cabbage, with their jerk chicken to complete their meal. Click on the picture to try the recipe!

Famous Dessert Dish: Hummingbird Cake

Hummingbird Cake was invented in Jamaica and is now a very popular desert in the United States. This cake has bananas, pineapples, pecans and many other ingredients to create a delicious cake, topped with cream cheese frosting.
